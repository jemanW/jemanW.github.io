Group axioms

   
$$ \forall a, b \in G, \exists c \in G \text{ s.t. } a \cdot b = c $$ $$ \forall a, b, c \in G, (a \cdot b) \cdot c = a \cdot (b \cdot c) $$ $$ \exists e \in G \text{ s.t. } \forall a \in G, e \cdot a = a \cdot e = a $$ $$ \forall a \in G, \exists b \in G \text{ s.t. } a \cdot b = b \cdot a = e $$
